Mobile Power!

The next generation handheld OS
for Smartphones

After a very tight competition for cellphones, NOW were in the competition for Smartphones
ANDROID, WINDOWS 8, and IOS (an Apple Iphone/Ipad Operating System) are widely used for many users of the world.
In addition of it's compactibilty, efficiency, portability, and usability, Many developers worldwide
are developed many types of software which it is called "Apps". Smartphones are not just used for Productibility,
it's hard to deny that some Smartphones are intillectually build for Games. According to Google Play (Android Market),
over 300,000 of apps are available in the market, while the Apple has also a hundreds of thousands of apps to choose from.
There's a app that you can make presentations, spreadsheets, business cards, and documents on the fly! There's a app that
makes me some silly like SPY Camera.

Tablets are recommended for full entertainment. The Leading Mobile Game Companies like Gameloft, Glu Mobile, EA Mobile,
and COMPUS are making many games to enjoy their Smartphones and Tablets. One of the usual game hits was "Angry Birds"
from small company "Rovio Entertainment". Angry Birds is now a usual game for those who have purchased the Phones today.
How can you tell that inspite of slow speed of processor (equivalent to Pentium III), small usage of RAM, and ultra fast
Usage, Games are too many to play for just Smartphones. I have my favorite Game for Android (should be available for IOS) like
Asphalt 5 and Modern Combat from Gameloft. Because of "Touch-Screen" interface, some games does'nt suit for Small Smartphones
or maybe it's esppecially made for tablets. Also my favorite was Tap Tap from Tapolous. You can play with latest hit songs
all over the world. But because of Expensive prize, maybe you can't afford to play harder.

Music and Videos is a major intention for some Smartphone users. Including Bluetooth, and Wi-Fi. Smartphones is just like a
computer. You will realize that is the same but the MOBILITY of SMartPHONES is most powerful than laptops and netbooks.
